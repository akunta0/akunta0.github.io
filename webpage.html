Grey's Anatomy

Plot Summary

The series follows Meredith Grey, the daughter of an esteemed general surgeon named Ellis Grey, following her acceptance into the residency program at the fictional Seattle Grace Hospital, (later named Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital). During her time as a resident, Grey works alongside fellow physicians Cristina Yang, Izzie Stevens, Alex Karev, and George O'Malley, who each struggle to balance their personal lives with hectic schedules and stressful residency requirements. During their internship, they are overseen by Miranda Bailey, a senior resident who works with attending physician Derek Shepherd, head of neurosurgery and Meredith's love interest; Preston Burke, head of cardiology, who becomes Yang's fiance; and Richard Webber, Chief of Surgery and attending general surgeon, the previous lover of Ellis Grey. During the first six seasons, Burke, O'Malley, and Stevens all depart the series.

Selected Main Characters

Meredith Grey is an award winning general surgeon, the current Chief of General Surgery, and a board member of Grey-Sloan Memorial. She was married to Derek Shepherd, an award winning nuerosurgeon who died due to a brain injury, and has 3 kids: Zola, Bailey, and Ellis.

Alex Karev is the current Chief of Pediatric Surgery and was the former interim Chief of Surgery. He is currently married to Jo Wilson, a surgical fellow.

Miranda Bailey is the current Chief of Surgery, a general surgeon, and a board member of Grey-Sloan Memorial. She is married to a surgeon turned firefighter Ben Warren and has one son: Tuck.

Richard Webber is a general surgeon, the former Chief of Surgery, and a board member of Grey-Sloan Memorial. He is married to an award winning urologist, Catherine Fox, and is the biological father of Maggie Pierce, the chief of Cardiothorasic surgery.

Jackson Avery is a plastic surgeon and the Chairman of the board of directors of Grey-Sloan Memorial. He is currently dating Maggie Pierce and has one daughter: Harriet.

Cristina Yang was a Cardiothorasic surgical fellow and a former board member of Grey-Sloan Memorial. She left the show at the end of season 10 and moved to Switzerland. She was married to Owen Hunt, a trauma surgeon and the former Chief of Surgery.

Derek Shepherd was an award winning neurosurgeon and a former board member of Grey-Sloan Memorial. He was married to Meredith Grey and had 3 kids: Zola, Bailey, and Ellis. He was the older brother to 4 younger sisters, one of whom, Amelia Shepard, was a fellow neurosurgeon. He died due to a brain injury from a car accident at the end of season 11.

Arizona Robbins was a former fetal and Pediatric surgeon at Grey Sloan Memorial and was also a board member of the hospital. She was married to Callie Torres, who moved to New York at the end of season 12. They have a daughter, Sophia, who's biological father was Mark Sloan. Robbins left the show at the end of season 14 and also moved to New York.

April Kepner was a trauma surgeon and the former interim Chief of General Surgery for a short period of time. She left the show at the end of season 14 to help homeless communities with little access to medical care. She has one daughter, Harriet, and is married to Mathhew Taylor, a paramedic.

Maggie Pierce is currently the Chief of Cardiothorasic surgery. She is Meredith Grey's half sister and the biological daughter of Richard Webber and Ellis Grey. She is currently dating Jackson Avery.
